// FAQ

FAQ

What file format should I use?+
The standard format is P,X,Y,Z,Code — one point per line. Comma or semicolon separator. Compatible with exports from most total stations and field software.
Can I use my own codes?+
Yes, on the Pro and Enterprise plans you can fully define your code table — name, type (line/symbol), layer, color and connection rules.
Is the generated DXF compatible with Civil 3D?+
Yes. The generated file is DXF R2010, compatible with AutoCAD, Civil 3D, BricsCAD and any software that reads DXF/DWG.
Is it possible to generate DWG?+
DWG is a proprietary Autodesk format. We generate DXF R2010 which AutoCAD opens and saves as DWG natively — the result is equivalent.
Is my data stored on your servers?+
No. Everything runs locally in your browser — no files are sent to any server. Complete privacy for your survey data.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes, no contracts or commitments. Cancel in your account settings and access continues until the end of the paid period.
